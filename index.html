Another one of my favorite thins are butterfly knifes

Butterfly knives

The butterfly knife is a type of folding pocketknife that originated in the Philippines. Its distinct features are two handles counter-rotating around the tang such that, when closed, the blade is concealed within grooves in the handles

I love to practice and learn new tricks with a butterfly knife, it is very fun, calming, and it looks really cool
